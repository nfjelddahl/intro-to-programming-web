The NAENAES

Who are we?

We are a pop band of four from Boston, Massachusetts. Neena the lead vocalist, John the guitarist, Jen the drummer, and Bill the bassist. We blend pop melodies with the alternative and rock sounds of electric guitars and snappy drums.
